About BGC

Bonifacio Global City is a 240-hectare mixed-use district in Taguig, Metro Manila, known as a major financial and lifestyle hub.

It is home to numerous multinational corporations, high-end residential towers, schools, and commercial establishments like restaurants, shops, and parks.

Whether you're here for food, recreation, or exploration, BGC welcomes everyone.
